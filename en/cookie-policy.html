Cookie Policy

1. What is a Cookie?

A cookie is a small text file downloaded onto your browser (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when accessing certain websites. Cookies allow a website to store and retrieve information about the browsing habits of a user or their device securely.

2. Declaration of Absence of Commercial Tracking

At ProCloud we strictly value our users' and clients' privacy. Therefore, we declare that on this website:

  • We DO NOT use advertising cookies for profiling or serving personalized ads.
  • We DO NOT use commercial tracking cookies or third-party pixels (such as Google Analytics, Facebook pixel, LinkedIn Insights, or other invasive tracking systems).
  • All browsing on this website is completely anonymous and is never tracked commercially.

3. Mandatory Technical Cookies Used

The only category of cookies that may be downloaded to your device is Mandatory Technical and Security Cookies, strictly required for the correct operation and security of the website. These cookies are associated with the following service:

  • Web3Forms / hCaptcha or form anti-spam system: To prevent automated spam submissions and protect the contact form from bots and attacks, a mandatory temporary technical cookie from hCaptcha is loaded. It verifies that form interactions are performed by real humans. This cookie is not used for advertising or tracking.

4. Managing and Disabling Cookies

Users can revoke their consent, configure, block, or delete cookies installed on their device by managing the settings of their browser:

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data.
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data.
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions.
  • Apple Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Block all cookies.

Please note that if you block the mandatory technical cookies from hCaptcha, the contact form may not submit correctly due to anti-spam security protocols.
