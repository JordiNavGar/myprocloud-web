Can I use my current domain name?

Yes. We configure Google Workspace to run on your custom corporate domain (e.g., name@yourcompany.com) and handle all required DNS configurations.

How does co-authoring work in Google Docs?

With Google Docs and Sheets, multiple team members can open and edit the same document in real time, showing edits as they occur.

Yes. Drive files are hosted in Google's secure datacenters. We enforce security permissions to prevent unauthorized external access.
