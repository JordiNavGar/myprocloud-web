FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens to backups if a ransomware attack occurs?

We write backups to immutable Veeam cloud nodes. Once written, the backup files are read-only and cannot be altered or deleted by malware.

How often are backups executed?

Typically, full backups run automatically every night. We can configure more frequent sync cycles depending on your database load.

Do I need to invest in expensive hardware?

No. We optimize our firewall rules and backup protocols to run seamlessly on your current systems and routers.

Need to modernize your IT infrastructure?

Tell us what system you currently have and we will help you define a secure, scalable cloud, hybrid, or remote access solution.
