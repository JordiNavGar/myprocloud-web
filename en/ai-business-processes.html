Common use cases

Professional firm with remote users

Manual Processes vs AI Applied to Processes

Area Manual Processes AI Applied to Processes

Summarization Reading long email threads or lengthy documents, consuming valuable time. Instant generation of summaries for meetings, audio files, minutes and contracts.

Emails Drafting individual replies to frequent customer queries. Quick generation of professional, customized answers categorized by topic.
