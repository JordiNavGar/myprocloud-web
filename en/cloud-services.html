Detailed Technology Services

Hybrid Cloud & Servers

We move your applications and databases to a high-performance hybrid or cloud environment. Ideal for replacing old local servers without losing control.

  • VPS Servers: Guaranteed performance with NVMe drives.
  • Hybrid Cloud: Perfect mix of local and cloud infrastructure.
  • Virtualization: Advanced management with VMware and Hyper-V.

Remote Access & VPN

Enable your team to work from home or on the go just as if they were in the office, using secure encrypted connections and controlled access.

  • Enterprise-grade VPN: Encrypted SSL/IPSec tunnels.
  • Remote Desktop (RDP): Protected RDP with a secure gateway.
  • Multi-site Connection: Secure interconnection of offices and branches.

Microsoft 365 & Google Workspace

We modernize your company's email and collaboration tools. We migrate your email history and configure advanced security policies.

  • Email Systems: Exchange Online and professional Gmail.
  • Cloud Collaboration: Setup of Teams, SharePoint, and Google Drive.
  • Security Policies: Active MFA and anti-phishing protection.

ERP Publishing in the Cloud

We move your accounting and invoicing systems to a remote environment. Access your Sage, SAP, or A3 databases without any local installation.

  • Cloud ERP: Sage 50, 200, SAP, and corporate software.
  • Firms & Advisors: Dedicated environments for A3 software.
  • Protected Web Access: Safely published corporate applications.

Backup & Cybersecurity

We protect your company's data with automated backup plans, network firewalls, and immutable off-site copies against Ransomware.

  • Professional Cloud Backup: Veeam Backup technology and off-site replication.
  • Perimeter Security: Physical and cloud firewalls.
  • DNS & Web Protection: Advanced integration with Cloudflare.

Specialized Technical Support
