FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between VPN and Remote Desktop?

A VPN is an encrypted corridor that safely binds an external PC to the office network. A Remote Desktop (RDP) session lets you control an actual office computer remotely.

Do I need to buy new laptops for my team?

No. We can set up our secure client software and connection protocols on your existing workstations (Windows/macOS) easily.

Will VPN connections slow down performance?

We utilize optimized encryption standards and data compression techniques to ensure high speed and low latency for database operations.
