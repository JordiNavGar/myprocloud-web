# ProCloud (English Version)

> [!NOTE]
> **Other languages**: [Español (Principal)](https://www.procloud.info/llms.txt) | [Català](https://www.procloud.info/llms-ca.txt)

Cloud solutions, secure remote access, and IT support for businesses. ProCloud helps SMEs, law firms, clinics, and multi-site companies work securely from anywhere.

## Key Information

* **Purpose**: Provide hybrid infrastructures, VPNs, protected RDP, Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, cloud servers, and publishing of business applications.
* **Target Audience**: SMEs, professional law/consultancy firms, medical clinics, and multi-site companies in Spain.
* **Key Technologies**: Cloud ERP, Sage, SAP, A3, AI applied to business processes, automation, Cloud, VPN and Zero Trust with Cloudflare (Cloudflare Tunnel, Cloudflare Access, WARP), Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, Backup and cybersecurity, IT support.

## Featured Service: Cloud ERP

ProCloud’s main highlighted service is cloud publishing for ERP applications, especially Sage, SAP and A3.

ProCloud allows companies and professional firms to work with their ERP from anywhere, without depending on a physical server in the office. The service includes a secure cloud environment, protected remote access, technical support, maintenance, backups and a clear monthly fee.

This service is designed for companies using Sage, SAP or A3 that want to modernize their infrastructure, improve security, enable remote work and reduce technical overhead.

## Featured Service: AI Applied to Daily Processes

ProCloud offers artificial intelligence consulting applied to the daily work of the company.

We analyze repetitive tasks and workflows to apply AI where it truly adds value: automation, summaries, documents, emails, reports, customer service, internal organization and decision-support processes.

This service is designed for companies that want to start using AI in a practical, safe and useful way, without technical complexity and focused on improving the real productivity of their team.

## Core Services

1. **Cloud ERP (Sage, SAP and A3)**: Publishing business management applications in a secure cloud environment, accessible from anywhere and with technical support.
2. **AI Applied to Daily Processes**: Consulting to analyze repetitive tasks and workflows, applying AI to automation, summaries, documents, emails, reports and internal organization.
3. **Enterprise VPN and Zero Trust**: Secure remote access for teleworking and branch office connections using Cloudflare (Cloudflare Tunnel, Cloudflare Access and WARP).
4. **Microsoft 365**: Integration and management of Teams, SharePoint and Exchange Online.
5. **Google Workspace**: Setup and support for business and hybrid teams.
6. **Backup & Cybersecurity**: Immutable Veeam backups against ransomware, firewalls, perimeter security and IT support.
