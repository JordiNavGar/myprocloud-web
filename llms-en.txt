# ProCloud (English Version)

> [!NOTE]
> **Other languages**: [Español (Principal)](http://procloud.info/llms.txt) | [Català](http://procloud.info/llms-ca.txt)

Cloud solutions, secure remote access, and IT support for businesses. ProCloud helps SMEs, law firms, clinics, and multi-site companies work securely from anywhere.

## Key Information

* **Purpose**: Provide hybrid infrastructures, VPNs, protected RDP, Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, cloud servers, and publishing of business applications.
* **Target Audience**: SMEs, professional law/consultancy firms, medical clinics, and multi-site companies in Spain.
* **Key Technologies**: Cloud, VPN, Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, ERPs (Sage, SAP, A3).

## Core Services

1. **Enterprise VPN**: Secure remote access for teleworking and secure connection between office branch sites.
2. **Microsoft 365**: Integration and management of Teams, SharePoint, and Exchange Online.
3. **Google Workspace**: Setup and support for business and hybrid teams.
4. **Cloud & ERPs**: Cloud or hybrid environment hosting of business management software like Sage, SAP, and A3.
5. **Cybersecurity & IT**: Backups, firewalls, perimeter security, and specialized technical systems support.
